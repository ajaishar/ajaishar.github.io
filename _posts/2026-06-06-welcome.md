I'm starting this blog as a place to think out loud about the questions I keep
coming back to: how to make AI agents work in the messy core operations of
real businesses, what the right primitives for an agent platform look like, and
how the economics of inference shape downstream product decisions.  

Most of what I write here will be short, closer to lab notes than essays. This world changes quickly,
and our working model of where the world is going needs to adapt accordingly. 

I dabble in a lot of things. I love technology, innovation, mathematics. The ideas behind why the world is the way it is. 
Why do these technologies work the way they do? I think mathematics is an elegant langauge for describing it. I don't like messy abstractions
and side effects just make me uneasy. 

More coming soon.
