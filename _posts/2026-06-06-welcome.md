I'm starting this blog as a place to think out loud about the questions I keep
coming back to: how to make AI agents reliable in the messy core operations of
real businesses, what the right primitives for an agent platform look like, and
how the economics of inference shape every product decision downstream.

Most of what I write here will be short — closer to lab notes than essays. If a
post says something obviously wrong six months later, I'd rather leave it up and
mark the update than pretend I always knew.

More soon.
